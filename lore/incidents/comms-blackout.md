# Incident Report: Deep-Space Comms Blackout (2095-11-22)

**Classification:** Closed.

For 4 hours 17 minutes on 2095-11-22, Aether Station lost all high-gain
relay to JOSC Earthside ground. Cause: a solar particle event clipped the
station's primary antenna feed during a routine maintenance window.

## Sequence

- 09:04 — Mira-7 logs anomalous flux in feed line A; raises advisory.
- 09:08 — Antenna preamp trips offline. Volkov in EVA prep.
- 09:32 — Park orders the crew to "narrowband only" power profile.
- 11:14 — Mira-7 rebuilds the feed via the auxiliary array.
- 13:21 — High-gain restored. Earthside informed.

## Outcome

No injuries. Twelve hours of science data partially desynced; recovered
from on-board buffers. Volkov used the silence to reorganize his tool
locker, which he refers to as "the only good thing that came of it."

Hua, who arrived months later, has heard this story three times.
