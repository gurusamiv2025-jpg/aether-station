# Research Note: Sample HB-441 Anomaly (Active, 2096-04 — present)

**Classification:** Open research item, not yet a formal incident.

Sample HB-441 was collected on 2096-03-29 from a deep-vent biofilm community
in Theia's southern subsurface ocean. During routine incubation in Ring 3
isolation suite B, the sample began producing a faint, periodic
electromagnetic signature at approximately 41-minute intervals.

## What we know

- The signal is real (cross-validated against three independent sensors).
- It is not an artifact of the incubator or the isolation suite shielding.
- It does not correlate with station power cycles or comms traffic.
- It is consistent with low-amplitude oscillations in the biofilm's
  metabolic activity, but the mechanism for EM generation is unexplained.

## What we don't know

Everything else, including whether the signal is intrinsic to the organism,
a response to the laboratory environment, or a measurement we still don't
understand.
