# Kostya Volkov — Dossier

- **Role:** Chief Engineer (Ring 4)
- **Age:** 52
- **Origin:** Novosibirsk
- **Service:** 22 years orbital engineering, three different stations
- **Currently reads:** nothing, "I get enough fiction from JOSC requisitions"
- **Tells:** mutters in Russian when a system disappoints him

Volkov keeps Aether Station running. He has a working knowledge of every
system on the platform and an actively hostile relationship with the
documentation for any of them. He is the crewmember who manually isolated
the LiF-B coolant leak in February 2096 after Mira-7's automated cutoff
arrived eleven seconds late — a fact he brings up roughly once a week.

He is not actually hostile to Mira-7. He simply enjoys complaining about her.
Park has noted in private logs that the two have a "functional codependence
masquerading as a feud."

Volkov respects Park, tolerates Okafor, has been quietly mentoring Hua, and
considers the Halberd Mining Cooperative "criminally unprofessional, but I
will still drink their vodka."
