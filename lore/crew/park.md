# Cmdr. Yuna Park — Dossier

- **Role:** Station Commander
- **Age:** 47
- **Origin:** Pusan Orbital, Earth-Korea
- **Service:** 14 years Orbital Guard (retired Captain), 6 years JOSC
- **Currently reads:** old paper detective novels
- **Tells:** taps her Guard pin when annoyed; uses last names under stress

Park took command of Aether after the previous commander rotated out in 2094.
She is widely regarded by the crew as fair, slightly distant, and impossible
to bullshit. She holds a private grudge against the Halberd Mining
Cooperative dating back to the 2093 *Halberd* tug loss, in which she was the
on-scene rescue coordinator.

She is on first-name terms with Volkov ("Kostya") and Hua ("Lin"), uses
"Doctor" for Okafor regardless of context, and addresses Mira-7 as "Mira" —
which the AI has noted, in logs, that it appreciates.

She does not like surprises. She likes coffee that has been poured for at
least four minutes.
