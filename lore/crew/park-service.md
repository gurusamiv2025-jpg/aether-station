# Cmdr. Yuna Park — Orbital Guard Service Record (summary)

Park served 14 years in the Orbital Guard (2074-2088), retiring at the
rank of Captain. Highlights:

- **2076-2079** — Sub-lieutenant aboard the patrol cutter *Vigil*.
- **2080** — Awarded the Guard Commendation for the *Helios-VII* tanker
  rescue; reported 26 hours awake on station.
- **2082-2085** — XO of the cutter *Constance*. Two cargo-piracy
  interdictions, no shots fired.
- **2086** — Promoted to Captain; assigned to the *Halberd* sector
  liaison desk, where she became familiar with the Halberd Mining
  Cooperative's safety record.
- **2087** — On-scene rescue coordinator for the *Halberd-Three* tug
  loss (later cited in JOSC's 2093 Halberd incident report).
- **2088** — Honorable discharge; transitioned to JOSC liaison work,
  then onto Aether Station as commander in 2094.

Park does not display her commendation. She wears the pin.
