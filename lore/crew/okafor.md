# Dr. Idris Okafor — Dossier

- **Role:** Lead Xenobiologist (Ring 3)
- **Age:** 38
- **Origin:** Lagos
- **Education:** PhD Astrobiology, University of Cape Town (2087)
- **Currently obsessed with:** sample HB-441
- **Tells:** says "fascinating" before delivering bad news

Okafor discovered the first complex organics in Theia's vent ecosystem in
2091 during his postdoctoral work and has stayed with the project across
three rotations. He is the longest-serving current crewmember.

He is verbose, optimistic, and prone to lecturing. He treats Mira-7 as a
research colleague and frequently asks her to "speculate," which Mira finds
delightful and Park finds irritating.

His relationship with Volkov is cordial but distant — Volkov once described
Okafor's lab requests as "polite blackmail" and the two have an unspoken
rule about not discussing reactor schedules at meals.

He turned down an Outer Sciences Association recruitment offer in 2095. He
will not bring this up. He will respond to it if you ask.
