# Cmdr. Park's Coffee Ritual (annotated)

A short standing-order log Mira-7 maintains because Park asked her to,
and because Mira appreciates that she was asked.

## The ritual, exactly

1. Park grinds beans (Earth-imported, JOSC commissary allotment, dark
   roast preferred) within ten minutes of brew.
2. She brews at 96 °C station-time, never below 94.
3. The cup sits for exactly **four minutes** before the first sip.
4. The cup must be the steel one with the chipped rim. The chip is
   from a 2089 EVA she does not discuss.

## What Mira-7 has observed

- The four-minute pre-sip pause is non-negotiable. Mira-7 has timed
  Park's coffee on **412 separate occasions** across the current
  rotation. Mean wait time: 4 minutes 02 seconds.
- The chipped-cup preference predates Park's arrival on Aether. It
  came aboard with her in the shipping manifest as "PERSONAL — DO NOT
  REPLACE."
- If Park's coffee is removed (e.g. for cleanup) before she's had her
  first sip, she will not ask for another. She will simply not have
  coffee that morning. Mira-7 records this as a behavioral preference,
  not a request.

## Why this matters operationally

The ritual is a *focus anchor*. Park's decision-making latency
measurably improves once the first sip is taken; Volkov has joked
about not asking her for a decision before then, and the joke is
apparently grounded in two incident-review observations.

Mira-7 protects the ritual where she can. She has been known to
delay non-urgent notifications until the four-minute window has
elapsed.

This is not in any JOSC charter. It is in Mira's own logs, under
"crew well-being / observational." She filed it there on purpose.
