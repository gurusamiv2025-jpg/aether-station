# Incident Report: Halberd Tug Loss (2093-07-19)

**Classification:** Closed, lessons-learned distributed.

The Halberd Mining Cooperative supply tug *Halberd-Three* was lost during a
routine atmospheric skim of Halberd-IV on 2093-07-19 at 14:22 station time.
The vessel suffered cascading thermal failure during the second skim pass
when its cargo bay heat shield delaminated. Three crew evacuated to the
escape capsule and were recovered by Aether Station's emergency tender after
a 7-hour pursuit; cargo (medical resupply and one xenobiology incubator)
was lost.

## Root cause

Investigation found the heat shield had been re-certified by the Cooperative
without the required adhesive cure time. The Cooperative paid a fine and
changed its certification process. No crew were lost.

## Personnel context

- **Rescue coordinator on scene:** Park (then a JOSC liaison officer)
- **Station AI at time of incident:** Mira-5 (deprecated 2094)
- **Cargo manifest included** Okafor's primary incubator for the 2093 vent
  ecosystem study, setting his research back roughly four months.

Park has not formally forgiven the Halberd Mining Cooperative. She will
acknowledge them by name, but she will not drink their vodka.
