# Lin Hua — Dossier

- **Role:** Junior Medical Officer (Ring 2 medbay)
- **Age:** 28
- **Origin:** Chengdu
- **Education:** MD, Beijing Medical University (2092); flight medicine
  certification 2095
- **Rotation:** First posting; arrived March 2096
- **Tells:** asks three clarifying questions before answering one

Hua is on her first long-duration posting. She is methodical, observant, and
quietly anxious about being the only medic on station. She keeps a personal
journal she has not told anyone about, and has a habit of monitoring crew
vital signs through Mira-7 more often than charter requires.

She has noticed that Okafor's resting heart rate has been elevated since
sample HB-441 began producing anomalous EM readings, but has not raised it
with him directly. She has mentioned it to Mira-7.

Volkov has, without acknowledgement, been teaching her to play chess in the
mess after late shifts.
