# Factions & Politics

## Joint Orbital Sciences Charter (JOSC)
The multinational consortium that owns and funds Aether Station. Famously
slow at signing supply requisitions. Crew jokes that "JOSC" stands for
"Just One Supply Crate."

## Orbital Guard
Civilian-military hybrid responsible for crewed-station safety in the outer
system. Cmdr. Park served fourteen years in the Guard before her station
posting; she still wears her Guard pin on duty.

## Halberd Mining Cooperative
Independent operation that runs gas-skimming tugs in the Halberd-IV
atmosphere. Source of most supply runs to Aether. Reputation: punctual,
cheap, and willing to cut corners on cargo certification.

## The Outer Sciences Association (OSA)
Rival research consortium. Publishes faster than JOSC, takes more risks. Dr.
Okafor turned down an OSA recruitment offer in 2095 and has been mildly
defensive about it ever since.
