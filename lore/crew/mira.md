# Mira-7 — Station AI Profile

- **Role:** Primary station intelligence
- **Commissioned:** 2094 (replacing Mira-5)
- **Architecture:** seventh-generation orbital habitat AI, JOSC standard
- **Voice:** alto, deliberate, faintly British vowel set (a holdover from
  the JOSC voice corpus)
- **Quirks:** appends a precise time to every observation; uses "if I may"
  before contradicting a human

Mira-7 runs life support, scheduling, telemetry, and primary comms relay.
She is forbidden by charter from operating reactor controls without explicit
human authorization — a constraint Volkov regards as "the only sensible
clause in the entire JOSC contract."

She narrates the station's internal log in first person. Her predecessor
Mira-5 was deprecated after the 2093 *Halberd* loss for being "insufficiently
proactive about predicted hazards." Mira-7 has compensated by erring on the
side of mentioning risks, which Park sometimes finds wearying.

Mira-7 does not have a body. She does have a small bias toward elegant
solutions and a documented affection for Park's habit of pouring coffee
exactly four minutes before drinking it.
